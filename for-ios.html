Can I install a CapCut Mod APK directly on my iPhone?
No. APK files are Android packages and are completely incompatible with iPhones. iOS uses IPA files and requires apps to be signed with an Apple certificate. The three methods on this page are the correct ways to get CapCut premium features on iPhone — no APK is involved.
Will my iPhone be banned or blocked by Apple for using these methods?
No. Apple does not ban or block iPhones for sideloading or using free trials. Method 1 (Free Trial) is 100% within Apple's official system. Method 2 (AltStore) uses Apple's own signing mechanism with your own Apple ID. Method 3 (TrollStore) exploits a kernel-level bug — not recommended for users worried about warranty, but will not result in device banning.
Does the AltStore method work on iOS 17 and iOS 18?
Yes. AltStore works on all iOS versions including iOS 17 and iOS 18. In fact, in the EU, AltStore is now an officially recognised alternative marketplace under the EU Digital Markets Act. For non-EU users (including India), the standard AltServer method described in this guide works on all iOS versions.
I'm on iOS 17. Which method is best for me?
For iOS 17, we recommend Method 1 (Official App + Free Trial) if you edit occasionally, or Method 2 (AltStore) if you edit regularly and want permanently unlocked features. TrollStore does NOT support iOS 17 or 18 — skip that method entirely.
Will the AltStore-installed CapCut stop working after 7 days?
Only if you use a free Apple ID (not a paid developer account). AltStore automatically refreshes the signature every 7 days as long as AltServer is running on your computer and both devices are on the same Wi-Fi. If you miss a refresh, the app will stop working temporarily — just reconnect and AltStore will re-sign it in seconds.
Is CapCut banned in India? Can I use it on iPhone?
The original CapCut ban in India (2020) was lifted and the app is currently available on the Indian App Store. As of 2025, CapCut is accessible in India. If you ever find it unavailable in your App Store region, you can change your App Store region to the US or access it via the methods above.
