How We Verify Safety

Our 5-Step APK Verification Process

We don’t just run a VirusTotal scan and call it done. Every APK goes through a rigorous 5-step process before it appears on our site. This is what makes us different from every other APK site in India.

Step 1 — Source Acquisition: We source mod APKs from trusted developers with verified histories and cross-reference against multiple sources.

Step 2 — Static Analysis: We decompile and inspect the APK code for hidden payloads, suspicious permissions, or data exfiltration routines.

Step 3 — VirusTotal Scan: Full scan across all 72 antivirus engines. We only publish APKs that score 0 detections.

Step 4 — Device Testing: Installation and functional testing on a minimum of 10 popular Indian Android devices per release.

Step 5 — SHA-256 Publication: We generate and publish a SHA-256 checksum so you can independently verify file integrity before installing.
